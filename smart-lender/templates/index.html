About Smart Lender AI

Smart Lender AI is an advanced, student engineering project built to demonstrate machine learning logic in finance. The platform evaluates credit applicant parameters—like dependents count, work type, annual income, loan principal requirements, and CIBIL scores. By running classification algorithms, it delivers high-fidelity risk breakdowns to streamline credit evaluation processes.
